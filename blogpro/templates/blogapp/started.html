Here are 3 simple steps to get you started!


Step 1:


Click on "Register" and register yourself.
After registering click on "Login", enter your username and pasword to login into your profile.

Step 2:

After Login you will be redirected to Home page.
Click on "New Post" to write your Blog.

Step 3:


After saving your Blog you will be redirected to Home page.
Click on "Drafts" to see your Blog. Select any of your blog from the list
You can edit, delete and publish your Blog.
Once your Blog is published, it will be shown in the Home page and will be visible by all users.
Viewer can comment on your Blog. And you as a user have right to approve and delete comments.
You can also comment on published blogs even though you are not logged in.

Keep Blogging :)
